Articles Entertainment Tips & Tricks

1. Stay hydrated. Your body needs to be able to focus on school work, not on how to do so you survive and not get dehydrated. If you do this, you won’t feel the need to take a break and start scrolling on your phone. This takes me to the second tip.

2. Take breaks, but take them in a smart way. Let your brain not have to accumulate any information. Social media means a lot of useless information that will fill your mind for no reason. Instead, you could go get a snack or listen to a song or do both at the same time.

3. Get enough sleep and don’t go to bed with your phone near. It’s going to make you want to start using it and that’s not what we want. We want to sleep because we have work to do.

4. Go for a walk. Fresh air can definitely help clear your mind and put order in your thoughts. Just enjoy the surroundings, without posting about it on Instagram stories.

5. The toughest of the toughest advice would be to delete all social media apps from the phone. It can be hard to be detached from all that’s going on and you can feel like you’re missing out. In reality, it will help your sanity in the long run. It would also make it more difficult to get distracted because you pick up your phone by instinct to check social media but whoops…it’s not there anymore. Back to studying.
