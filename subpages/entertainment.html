Social media friends

"Social media friends" is a short film/montage which portrays some situations in which social media infiltrates and creates an invisible wall between us and the people around us. It's centered around a character which is addicted to their phone. The video follows them as they prepare for the day, hang out with a friend and try to study. In the end, they end up not doing either of those properly.
