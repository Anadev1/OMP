What is social media?

Social media represents an online medium where people can create communities, share their own content ot other people's content or just communicate. It can be used by private persons for entertainment, finding information or just watching cat video. It could also be used by businesses to promote their product or service, which is getting more on more popular, as we can observe all the ads that appear on our devices all the time.

What is the purpose of the magazine?

The purpose of the magazine is to present the influence social media has on people today in a easy and fun way to digest it. The idea was to research how college students use social media in their everyday life and with this information, create content that can entertain them, but also help them have a more productive life.
